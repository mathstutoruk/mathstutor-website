Bio

Academic background, teaching experience and the philosophy behind my approach to mathematics tuition.

Academic Credentials

I completed a Bachelor of Science (BSc Hons) at Loughborough University in 2016 with a strong focus on pure mathematics. My studies developed rigorous logical thinking, formal mathematical reasoning and the ability to approach complex problems with clarity and precision.

My academic background also includes A-level studies in Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics and Philosophy. I achieved 93% in AS-level Philosophy one year early during GCSE studies, placing among the highest-performing students nationally.

Teacher Training

I undertook teacher training in Secondary Mathematics at Brunel University, gaining experience across inner-city and diverse school settings including state and independent colleges. This developed strong classroom management, clear explanation and effective lesson structure.

Teaching Approach

I prioritise clarity, structure and active engagement. Complex ideas are broken into manageable steps, with lessons adapted to each student’s thinking style. My aim is genuine understanding rather than short-term memorisation.

Practical Experience

Alongside my degree, I completed the Employability Award at Loughborough University, supporting students from disadvantaged backgrounds. This strengthened rapport-building, adaptability and the ability to help learners make meaningful progress.

What Students Can Expect

Students can expect a calm, focused and supportive environment where progress is built step by step. Whether aiming for higher grades, stronger confidence or deeper understanding, lessons are tailored carefully to long-term success.
