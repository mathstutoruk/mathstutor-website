Bio

Surinder Sangha

A mathematics tutor with a strong academic background, teacher training in Secondary Mathematics, and experience across classroom teaching and one-to-one tuition. The focus throughout is on clarity, careful structure and helping students develop genuine mathematical understanding.

Academic Credentials

My academic background includes A-level studies in Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics and Philosophy, providing a strong foundation across both analytical and conceptual disciplines. I later completed a BSc (Hons) in Mathematics at Loughborough University in 2016, with a particular focus on pure mathematics, logical reasoning and formal problem solving. AS-level Philosophy was completed one year early during GCSE studies, achieving 93% — the highest mark nationally.

Teacher Training & Classroom Experience

I undertook teacher training in Secondary Mathematics at Brunel University, gaining experience across a range of inner-city and diverse school settings, including both state schools and independent colleges.

Teaching Approach

My teaching approach combines clarity, structure and active engagement. Complex ideas are broken down into manageable steps, students are encouraged to ask questions, and lessons are shaped around genuine understanding rather than memorisation.
